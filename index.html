My name is Emily Baik and I am a Student Advisor for the UCR Financial Wellness Office. Currently, I manage the social media aspect to help promote financial literacy in UCR students. Students are welcome to ask questions, attend Zoom meetings, and even request private counselling! As someone who grew up in a low-income family, financial literacy has been an incredibly important asset to me my whole life. Without a strong foundation in money management, how can you expect to grow and improve your situation? I owe much of where I am today financially to those who were willing to teach me and find a strong drive to help others in their own financial journeys as others have done for me.

Another job I currently work is private tutoring. With Covid keeping many students away from physical classrooms, it is my job to keep students motivated and on track! I primarily work with elementary students on their math, reading, and language arts. Teaching has always been a passion of mine and it fulfills me to see my students succeed. I started teaching music theory and the violin in middle school and gradually found myself falling in love with teaching in general. Now as a student advisor and private tutor, I am able to pursue my passions and continue to practice what I love.

Education is an incredibly important subject to me and I hope to see many of the organizations I work with reflect that similar passion for knowledge.
